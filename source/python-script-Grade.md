## python Grade

Python example: Grade

```python
print("give me your grade percent and I will tell you your letter grade")
grade=eval(input("what is your grade? "))

if grade>=89:
    print("you have an A")
elif grade>79:
    print("you have a B")
elif grade>69:
    print("you have a C")
elif grade>59:
    print("you have a D")
else:
    print("you have a F")

```
